My Journey

Born and raised in the picturesque Pokhara Metropolitan City of Nepal, I developed a profound fascination with both the earth beneath our feet and the stars above our heads from an early age.

My academic journey in geology began in Nepal, where the majestic Himalayas served as my first natural laboratory. The unique geological formations of my homeland inspired me to pursue a deeper understanding of earth sciences.

Alongside my scientific pursuits, I was drawn to the ancient wisdom of Krishnamurti Paddhati (KP) astrology, finding remarkable connections between celestial patterns and earthly events.

My quest for knowledge eventually led me to the United States, where I continue to bridge the gap between modern scientific understanding and traditional astrological wisdom, offering insights that honor both perspectives.
